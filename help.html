Pig Dice Game Help

Instructions

  1. Enter the resspective names of the players in the form
  2. Click on the Start game button
  3. Click on Roll Dice to play
  4. Click on the Hold button to give a chance to the next player

About

This game Pig Dice allows a player to roll a dice with his or her scores adding cumulatively per dice roll. When satisfied with score, a player clicks on the Hold button to give chance to the next player. When a player rolls on one, he or she scores a zero and the next player is given a chance to play! A player who get 100 points first is the winner
